Frequently Asked Questions

How long does setup take?

Once you complete our questionnaire (which takes 2–3 hours), we’ll build your AI twin within 3–5 working days. As soon as it’s ready, your creator chat page will be live and ready to launch.

Can I update my twin's personality?

Absolutely! Your AI twin should evolve with your fanbase. You can update your twin whenever you'd like by submitting new content or preferences.

What happens if I exceed my message limit?

We’ll send you a heads-up if you're getting close to your fair use message limit. If you're consistently over, we’ll reach out to discuss a custom plan that works for you.

Are there any ongoing costs?

No monthly fees. You only pay a one-time setup fee to build your AI twin. PersonaCore takes a 30% commission from any fan subscription revenue; the remaining 70% goes directly to you.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use Cloudflare's secure infrastructure and OpenAI’s API. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties.

What does the setup fee include?

The setup fee covers full AI twin creation, training using your custom questionnaire, and the launch of your personalized fan chat page. It’s a one-time cost with no hidden fees.

Can I remove or pause my AI twin?

Yes, you're always in control. If you ever want to pause or delete your twin, just contact us and we’ll take care of it promptly.

How do fans chat with my AI twin?

Fans can interact with your AI twin via your public chat page. Each message is designed to reflect your personality and tone, creating a unique fan experience.
