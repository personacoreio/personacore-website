2. The Data We Collect About You

We may collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of personal data about you which we have grouped together as follows:

  • Identity Data: first name, last name, username or similar identifier, and date of birth
  • Contact Data: billing address, delivery address, email address and telephone numbers
  • Financial Data: bank account and payment card details
  • Transaction Data: details about payments to and from you and other details of products and services you have purchased from us
  • Technical Data: internet protocol (IP) address, your login data, browser type and version, time zone setting and location
  • Profile Data: your username and password, purchases or orders made by you, your interests, preferences, feedback and survey responses
  • Usage Data: information about how you use our website, products and services
  • Marketing and Communications Data: your preferences in receiving marketing from us and our third parties
  • AI Training Data: personality traits, communication style preferences, content preferences, conversation history with AI twins
  • Conversation Data: all messages exchanged between fans and AI twins, conversation topics, sentiment analysis
  • Creator Profile Data: detailed personality questionnaires, content style preferences, expertise areas

4. Disclosures of Your Personal Data

We may share your personal data with:

  • AI Service Providers: We share Creator Profile Data and Conversation Data with AI service providers (including Anthropic) to train and operate AI personalities
  • Payment Processors: We share Financial and Transaction Data with Stripe for payment processing
  • Cloud Infrastructure: We use Cloudflare and Supabase for secure data storage and processing

5. International Transfers

We may transfer, store, and process your personal data outside the UK, including to the United States, through our trusted service providers:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): We use Anthropic's API services to power AI conversations. Your Conversation Data may be processed in the United States
  • Cloudflare: Our infrastructure uses Cloudflare's global network
  • Stripe: Payment processing may involve international data transfers

We ensure all international transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards, including Standard Contractual Clauses.

6. Data Security

We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used or accessed in an unauthorised way, altered or disclosed.

7. Data Retention

We will only retain your personal data for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfil the purposes we collected it for.

  • AI Training Data: Retained for as long as the creator account remains active, plus 2 years after account closure
  • Conversation Data: Retained for 3 years to maintain conversation context and improve AI performance
  • AI Model Data: When you delete your account, we will remove your personal data from active AI models within 30 days

8. Your Legal Rights

Under data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of your personal data
  • Request erasure of your personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Request transfer of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

9. AI-Specific Provisions

How We Use AI

We use artificial intelligence to create digital twins of creators that can interact with fans. This involves:

  • Personality Training: Using Creator Profile Data to train AI models to mimic creator personalities
  • Conversation Processing: Using Conversation Data to provide realistic interactions
  • Continuous Improvement: Analyzing interactions to improve AI accuracy

Your Control Over AI Data

  • Creators can update or delete their AI training data at any time
  • Fans can delete their conversation history
  • Both creators and fans can request export of their AI-related data

AI Limitations

Our AI twins are trained to mimic personalities but are not sentient. They may occasionally produce inaccurate or unexpected responses. We continuously monitor and improve AI performance.
